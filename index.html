Bio

Tim has performed bluegrass, celtic, classical, rock, jazz, Klezmer, folk, fusion, country, pop, rock, and funk all over the Midwest. Tim composes in a variety of traditional and modern genres. His compositions include: full orchestrations, music for chamber orchestras, jazz combos, choral pieces, folk, pop, country, rock, funk, hip-hop songs and arrangements, instrumental arrangements, underscoring and source music, and pieces for solo instrument and voice. He has composed music for film and commercials. He recently provided original jazz piano tracks for the Katherine Keener/Jeanne Tripplehorn feature, "Little Pink House". Tim can be found writing music, at home cooking, or performing around town with a wonderful variety of talented musicians.

Tim studied guitar with Billy Barnard (Count Basie/Rufus Reid/Duke Ellington) while attending U of M - Duluth, where he majored in Jazz Studies and Theory/Composition. He studied composition with Dr. Tom Wegren (student of Beveridge Webster and Nadia Boulanger) and Dr. Justin Rubin. He studied Jazz and Theory privately with Paul Renz(Berklee, New England Conservatory) as well as voice with Matt Culloton, composer and Artistic Director and Conductor of The Singers (formerly the Dale Warland Singers).

Check out Tim's performance schedule by clicking on the calendar tab in the menu.

Tim teaches privately in south Minneapolis at The Music Lab in the Nokomis and Hale neighborhoods.
